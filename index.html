What is Social Wifi?

Do you have WiFi? That’s a question you probably often heard as an entrepreneur! Therefor Ravonet has developed a handy marketing tool that allows you to deliver Social WiFi on your own WiFi network! Your client can make free use of your WiFi network by accessing the Pling Social WiFi hotspot by only one simple action. For example, by liking your company’s Facebook, by entering their email address, following you on Twitter or viewing your personal action page.

When a client connects to Social WiFi it will, upon connection, show your personal splash page. Then, the visitor can choose on which button to click to be free of charge connected with your Social WiFi.
